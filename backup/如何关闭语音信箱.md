
# 人工渠道
1.打开 https://support2.giffgaff.com/app/ask

2.点击 `Show more`

![KiJ6rXcYRaBIsCKhEc4IxkAVvcMND4WV.webp](https://cdn.nodeimage.com/i/KiJ6rXcYRaBIsCKhEc4IxkAVvcMND4WV.webp)

3.选择 `Account Details, settings and features`

![TymApXOgtvwJcWUzSlCxBEEDY2GK66Im.webp](https://cdn.nodeimage.com/i/TymApXOgtvwJcWUzSlCxBEEDY2GK66Im.webp)

4.选择自己账户

5.点击 `Enabling or disabling services（adult content,service bars,roaming）`

![2XlIf7ASjx5vJkyiRLZi3cwCoLRPnjnI.webp](https://cdn.nodeimage.com/i/2XlIf7ASjx5vJkyiRLZi3cwCoLRPnjnI.webp)

6.点击 `Call & texts`

![cPQX7NlYui7dsDvghjA8h3YYikhViY04.webp](https://cdn.nodeimage.com/i/cPQX7NlYui7dsDvghjA8h3YYikhViY04.webp)

7.点击 `Roming/international calls and texts`

![6DqV9HRcs6romnLlMhscnOI91ZFIFYQZ.webp](https://cdn.nodeimage.com/i/6DqV9HRcs6romnLlMhscnOI91ZFIFYQZ.webp)

8.点击 `No, I still need help from an agent`

![JP7ikCn581g8OHGsW3cJTCwsSghljzYI.webp](https://cdn.nodeimage.com/i/JP7ikCn581g8OHGsW3cJTCwsSghljzYI.webp)

9.在信息栏输入（机翻，有条件可以自行编辑）

`您好，我目前正在外出旅行。麻烦帮我关闭语音信箱服务，以免产生额外费用，非常感谢。`

> Hello, I'm currently travelling outside the UK. Could you please help me turn off my voicemail service completely to avoid any unexpected roaming charges? Thank you very much.

![Pb68DoDbJhx2jRCL25r5ZsZrlHwOzO7b.webp](https://cdn.nodeimage.com/i/Pb68DoDbJhx2jRCL25r5ZsZrlHwOzO7b.webp)

10.一般等待24小时左右客服回复就成功了。

![ACkMXE6FySPlcLzR98uYlaMKfO72FKhs.webp](https://cdn.nodeimage.com/i/ACkMXE6FySPlcLzR98uYlaMKfO72FKhs.webp)
